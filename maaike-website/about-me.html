I am a Dutch-Canadian singersongwriter, who has traveled all over the world. I am singing, playing the guitar. My music often has a feminine tone, but is full of her hidden thoughts, confessions and broken hearts, so that you remain moved by my music.
KNOW ME
In recent years, I have been traveling a lot. I love challenging myself and doing things that make me feel alive. Music has always been my hobby since I was very little. I love playing the guitar and meeting new people that share my perspective on things.
HOBBIES
I feel very inspired by the nature and everything around me. Traveling makes me feel alive and I love connecting with the world. What really inspires me is when I can actually make a change with my music and touch others. I feel very energised when I can connect with my audience and share my thoughts and emotions.
